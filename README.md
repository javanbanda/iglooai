# iglooai
Igloo is an AI-powered lecture note-taking platform built by Javan Ban to help students capture, understand, and organize information effortlessly.


Designed with a clean, intelligent, and modern interface, Igloo combines real-time speech transcription with advanced AI features to transform lectures into structured, actionable notes. Inspired by the simplicity of Notion and the intelligence of Otter.ai, the experience is calm, focused, and productivity-driven.

### Features

* 🎙️ Real-time lecture transcription
* 🧠 AI-generated summaries
* 📌 Automatic key-point extraction
* 📂 Smart note organization and categorization
* 🔍 Searchable lecture history
* ❄️ Minimal Arctic-inspired design system

Igloo turns hours of lectures into concise, organized knowledge, helping students spend less time taking notes and more time learning.
